## What Matters Most For This Decision

This hire is not primarily about:

- culture fit
- personality
- broad résumé strength
- interest in the mission

This hire is primarily about:

- speed with judgment
- ability to finish an inherited codebase safely
- comfort with billing and workflow risk
- reliability in a part-time or constrained schedule
- quality of async execution

## Quick Read

### 1. Delivery Confidence

Rate 1 to 5:

- Can they take over a mature codebase without a long ramp?
- Can they identify the shortest safe path to launch?
- Can they commit to concrete weekly deliverables?
- Can they move fast without destabilizing critical flows?

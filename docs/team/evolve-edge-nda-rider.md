# Evolve Edge NDA Rider

**Evolve Edge Confidentiality, Security, and AI Use Rider**

This Rider supplements and controls, solely with respect to Evolve Edge, any confidentiality, nondisclosure, contractor, staffing, recruiting, or services agreement through which a contractor, consultant, vendor, intermediary, or recruiting platform personnel (collectively, "Recipient") may receive access to Evolve Edge information. In the event of a conflict between this Rider and the underlying agreement, this Rider controls as to Evolve Edge Confidential Information and Work Product.

1. **Confidential Information.** "Confidential Information" includes all nonpublic information relating to Evolve Edge, its affiliates, customers, prospects, vendors, systems, operations, products, pricing, security, compliance, audits, reports, source code, object code, architecture, data models, prompts, workflows, documentation, credentials, secrets, API keys, tokens, incident information, and all customer or prospect information in any form, including extracts, summaries, and derivatives.

2. **Permitted Use.** Recipient may use Confidential Information solely to perform services expressly authorized in writing by Evolve Edge and for no other purpose. Recipient shall not disclose Confidential Information except to personnel with a strict need to know who are bound by written obligations at least as protective as this Rider. The intermediary vendor remains responsible for all acts and omissions of any contractor, subcontractor, or other personnel it places or manages.

3. **Security and Incident Notice.** Recipient shall maintain reasonable and appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including multi-factor authentication, encrypted devices and storage, access controls, and secure transmission methods. Recipient shall not share credentials or permit unauthorized access. Recipient shall notify Evolve Edge promptly, and in no event later than twenty-four (24) hours after discovery, of any actual or suspected unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, security incident, or compromise involving Evolve Edge Confidential Information, and shall cooperate fully in remediation.

4. **AI and Data Restrictions.** Recipient shall not use Evolve Edge Confidential Information, customer information, prompts, workflows, outputs, reports, schemas, or datasets to train, fine-tune, benchmark, improve, or ground any public, shared, third-party, or internal AI or machine learning model except with Evolve Edge's prior written consent. Recipient shall not reuse such information or outputs for any other client, product, portfolio, case study, or marketing purpose.

5. **Work Product and Third-Party Materials.** All work product created, conceived, reduced to practice, or delivered in connection with the services for Evolve Edge, including code, documentation, configurations, analyses, prompts, reports, workflows, and related materials, shall be owned exclusively by Evolve Edge and assigned to Evolve Edge upon creation. Recipient shall not incorporate any preexisting materials, open-source software, models, libraries, or other third-party materials into work product without prior written disclosure to and approval by Evolve Edge.

6. **Return, Deletion, and Publicity.** Upon request or termination of access or services, Recipient shall immediately cease use of Confidential Information and return or securely destroy all Confidential Information, credentials, and copies, and certify such return or destruction upon request. Recipient shall not identify Evolve Edge as a client or disclose the existence or nature of the engagement in any resume, website, social media post, proposal, or other public statement without Evolve Edge's prior written consent.

7. **Remedies; Survival.** Any limitation of liability in the underlying agreement shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality, misuse of data, security incidents caused by Recipient, infringement, fraud, willful misconduct, or violations of this Rider's AI-use restrictions. Evolve Edge is entitled to injunctive and equitable relief in addition to any other remedies. These obligations survive termination, and trade secret obligations survive for so long as applicable law protects the information as a trade secret.

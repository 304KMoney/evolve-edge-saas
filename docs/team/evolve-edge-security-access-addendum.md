# Evolve Edge Security & Access Addendum

**Evolve Edge Security and Access Addendum**

This Addendum supplements any confidentiality, nondisclosure, contractor, staffing, recruiting, or services agreement under which a contractor, consultant, vendor, intermediary, or recruiting platform personnel (collectively, "Recipient") may access Evolve Edge systems, data, environments, or accounts. In the event of a conflict, this Addendum controls as to security, access, handling of Evolve Edge data, and incident response obligations.

1. **Least-Privilege Access.** Recipient shall receive only the minimum access reasonably necessary for the approved services. Access rights are personal, may not be shared, and may be modified or revoked by Evolve Edge at any time.

2. **Account Security.** Recipient shall use unique accounts, strong passwords, and multi-factor authentication for all Evolve Edge systems and any supporting services used to access Evolve Edge information. Shared logins are prohibited unless expressly approved in writing by Evolve Edge for a specific operational purpose.

3. **Device and Environment Security.** Recipient shall access Evolve Edge systems only from devices and environments using current operating system and security updates, full-disk encryption, screen-lock controls, and commercially reasonable anti-malware protections. Public or insecure devices and networks may not be used unless protected by approved secure access controls.

4. **Data Handling Restrictions.** Recipient shall access, use, store, copy, transmit, and retain Evolve Edge information only as necessary for the approved services. Recipient shall not move Evolve Edge data into personal accounts, unapproved tools, unapproved storage locations, or unapproved AI systems. Production data may not be downloaded locally except where strictly necessary and expressly authorized by Evolve Edge.

5. **Customer Data and Regulated Information.** If Recipient may access customer data, personal data, security materials, compliance materials, or audit-related information, Recipient shall follow Evolve Edge's written handling instructions and shall not use such information for testing, demonstrations, portfolio materials, training datasets, or any purpose outside the approved services.

6. **Change Controls and Third Parties.** Recipient shall not create new integrations, sub-processors, external shares, forwarding rules, repositories, environments, or access pathways involving Evolve Edge information without prior written approval. Recipient shall not grant access to any subcontractor or third party unless expressly approved in writing by Evolve Edge and bound by equivalent written obligations.

7. **Logging, Cooperation, and Monitoring.** Recipient acknowledges that Evolve Edge may log, monitor, audit, restrict, or suspend access to its systems and accounts for security, compliance, and operational purposes. Recipient shall reasonably cooperate with access reviews, security questionnaires, audit requests, incident investigations, and remediation steps.

8. **Incident Reporting.** Recipient shall report any actual or suspected unauthorized access, disclosure, credential compromise, malware event, lost device, phishing event, misdirected communication, or other security incident affecting Evolve Edge information immediately, and in no event later than twenty-four (24) hours after discovery. Recipient shall preserve relevant evidence, mitigate further risk, and cooperate fully with Evolve Edge.

9. **Return of Access; Deletion.** Upon request, role change, suspension, or termination of services, Recipient shall immediately stop using Evolve Edge systems and information, return or destroy all credentials, tokens, keys, devices, and copies in Recipient's possession or control, and certify completion upon request. Evolve Edge may revoke access without prior notice where reasonably necessary for security.

10. **Remedies and Survival.** Recipient's obligations under this Addendum survive termination for so long as Recipient retains or has access to Evolve Edge information and thereafter as required by the underlying agreement or applicable law. Breach of this Addendum may cause irreparable harm for which equitable relief is appropriate in addition to any other remedies.

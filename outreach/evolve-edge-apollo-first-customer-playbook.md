# Evolve Edge Apollo First-Customer Playbook

## Goal

Get the first paying customer for Evolve Edge by selling a narrow, urgent service-led offer:

**AI Audit Readiness Sprint**

Promise: organize a company's AI governance, customer security review answers, and audit evidence package in 10 business days.

## Apollo Lists

Create or import into these Apollo lists:

- `Evolve Edge - Priority - AI SaaS First Customer`
- `Evolve Edge - Secondary - Compliance and HIPAA`

Local exports:

- `outreach/evolve-edge-apollo-ai-saas-priority-prospects.csv`
- `outreach/evolve-edge-apollo-first-customer-prospects.csv`

## Priority ICP

Target first:

- AI-enabled B2B SaaS companies
- 11-500 employees
- US, Canada, UK
- Founder, CEO, COO, CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering
- Head of Security, Head of Compliance, GRC Manager when present
- Signs of enterprise sales, SOC 2 pressure, trust center, Vanta, Drata, vendor risk, customer security reviews

Secondary:

- Healthtech and HIPAA-sensitive operators
- IT directors, security officers, compliance managers
- Use this if SaaS founder outreach is too slow or if the offer is reframed around HIPAA AI governance evidence

## Scoring

Prioritize prospects with:

- Verified email available in Apollo
- Founder/CEO/CTO/COO title
- AI, SaaS, software, platform, automation, security, compliance, or trust signals
- Recent Apollo refresh date
- Company size under 500 employees

Deprioritize:

- Universities, government, pure agencies, staffing firms
- Large enterprises with long procurement cycles
- Companies with no obvious AI, security, compliance, or enterprise sales pressure

## Apollo Search Filters

People search:

- Titles: Founder, Co-Founder, CEO, COO, CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, Head of Security, Head of Compliance, GRC Manager
- Company headcount: 11-200 and 201-500
- Company HQ: United States, Canada, United Kingdom
- Keywords: AI SaaS, AI software, AI platform, enterprise AI, SOC 2 SaaS, security questionnaire SaaS, trust center SaaS, Vanta SaaS, Drata SaaS

Use Apollo's UI to save the search and then review the top 50 manually before enriching emails.

## First Sequence

### Email 1

Subject: AI security reviews

Hi {{first_name}},

Quick question: are customers or prospects asking {{organization}} how you use AI in the product or internally?

I am working on Evolve Edge, which helps AI-enabled SaaS teams turn scattered AI policies, security answers, and audit evidence into a customer-ready package.

The first offer is a 10-business-day AI Audit Readiness Sprint. It is meant for teams getting pulled into SOC 2, vendor risk, or enterprise security reviews.

Worth a quick look this week?

### Email 2

Subject: Re: AI security reviews

Hi {{first_name}},

The pattern I am looking for is simple: a team ships AI features, then enterprise buyers start asking harder questions about data handling, model usage, governance, and audit evidence.

Most teams can answer the questions, but the proof is scattered across docs, Slack, tickets, vendor settings, and security tools.

Evolve Edge packages that into a cleaner control plane and evidence workflow.

Open to comparing notes for 15 minutes?

### Email 3

Subject: customer evidence

Hi {{first_name}},

One concrete thing we can help with:

Take your existing AI/security/compliance material and turn it into a reusable customer evidence pack:

- AI use policy
- customer security review answers
- vendor/model inventory
- audit-ready evidence checklist
- gaps and owner map

This is designed to be useful even before a full platform rollout.

Should I send over the sprint outline?

### Email 4

Subject: AI governance gap

Hi {{first_name}},

If AI governance is already handled at {{organization}}, no worries.

If it is living across ad hoc docs and one-off customer answers, that is exactly the gap Evolve Edge is built around.

I am looking for one early customer where we can make the workflow genuinely useful, not just polished.

Would a 10-day sprint be relevant?

### Email 5

Subject: close the loop

Hi {{first_name}},

Last note from me.

I am reaching out because {{organization}} looks like the kind of team that may hit AI governance and customer security review pressure earlier than most.

If that is not on your radar, I will close the loop.

If it is, I can send the AI Audit Readiness Sprint outline and a simple fixed-price option.

Worth sending?

## Call CTA

Use one CTA everywhere:

**"Worth a 15-minute look this week?"**

## Suggested Price

First customer:

- $1,500-$3,000 for the 10-business-day sprint
- Credit 100% of the sprint fee toward the first 3 months of Evolve Edge if they continue

Do not lead with a broad SaaS subscription. Lead with the sprint, then convert to platform usage.

## Next Step Before Apollo Mutation

Before saving/enriching net-new contacts or adding anyone to a sequence:

1. Manually review the top 50 in Apollo.
2. Remove poor-fit companies.
3. Enrich 25 contacts max.
4. Send low-volume, founder-style outreach first.
5. Track replies and objections before scaling.
